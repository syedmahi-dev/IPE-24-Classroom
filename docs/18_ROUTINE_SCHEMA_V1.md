## 3. Groups & Rotation Logic

| Group ID | Label          | Timetable Notation |
|----------|----------------|--------------------|
| G1       | Group 1 (Even) | Superscript ¹      |
| G2       | Group 2 (Odd)  | Superscript ²      |

### Rotation Rule
- **Week A** (1st week of rotation, starting Monday): G1 performs labs marked ¹; G2 performs labs marked ²
- **Week B** (2nd week): G1 and G2 **fully swap** all lab assignments
- The rotation resets at the beginning of each month (Monday = Day 1 of rotation)
